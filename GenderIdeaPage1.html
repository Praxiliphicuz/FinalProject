Lifestyle

Transgender people's lifestyles can vary widely, but they often involve expressing their gender identity in a way that is authentic to them. This can include social, legal, and medical transitions.

Compared to their cisgender peers, transgender and gender non-conforming people are more likely to experience employment discrimination, lack health insurance, live in poverty, face housing discrimination, be homeless, and lack access to health care. This doesn't deter them from being their true selves because we belive it's all worth it be your true self.
