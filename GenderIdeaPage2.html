Gender Spectrum Health

Transgender people have a few resources they can choose to access for theirselves. The name for this is called "Gender Affiriming Care". It refers too a range of medical, social, psychological interventions that support and affirm an individual's gender identity when it differs from the gender they were assigned at birth.

Administering hormones (estrogen or testosterone) to align physical characteristics with the individual's gender identity is one of of affirming medical choices that transgender persons do to feel more aligned with thieir true themselves. There are also a wide range of Surgical procedures to alter physical features, such as breast augmentation, chest reconstruction, genital surgery, facial feminzation Surgery such as Rhinoplasty and Forehead reduction surgery.
